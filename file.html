About NJcreation1313

Welcome to NJcreation1313!

I'm Navjot, a boutique designer based in Montreal, Canada. I grew up watching my mother stitch beautiful Punjabi suits — salwar suits, lehengas, shararas, and more — in our family-run boutique.

Inspired by her dedication, I now continue her legacy by creating customized and stylish Punjabi wear with love and attention to detail. Each piece is crafted to celebrate the rich heritage of Punjabi fashion while incorporating modern aesthetics.
